Flynn's Historic Battle

by watchfulwatcher - May 2nd 2020

Follow @watchfulwatcher

For more than three years, Micheal Flynn, a retired United States Army Lieutenant General who was briefly the National Security Advisor to President Donald Trump, waged a historic battle against a Shadow Government seeking to destroy Trump's presidency.



Flynn's case gives a rare insight into FBI extortion tactics and uncovered undeniable evidence of a Shadow Government attempting a coup to remove President Trump from office.



On December 1, 2017, Flynn appeared in federal court to formalize a deal with Special Counsel Robert Mueller to plead guilty to a felony count of "willfully and knowingly" making "false, fictitious and fraudulent statements" to the FBI in relation to the Special Counsel's investigation into Russian interference in the 2016 elections.



The FBI claim Flynn lied to them about conversations he had with then-Russian Ambassador to U.S. Sergey Kislyak in December 2016.



In January 2020, Flynn asked to withdraw his guilty plea, and submitted a formal written statement in court titled, "Declaration of Michael T. Flynn". His declaration proclaimed his innocence, and tells the story from his perspective, detailing how the FBI threatened his family.
