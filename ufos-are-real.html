UFOs Are Real but Who's Flying Them

by WatchfulWatcher - 2/4/2020

After almost a century of cover-ups and denial, the public now has enough evidence to confirm the existence of advanced aircraft capable of breaking the laws of known physics, leaving many to ask the question, who is flying these UFOs?



In the past three years alone the US Navy has confirmed the authenticity of the Nimitz Encounter UFO video, updated their guidelines for reporting on UFOs, and patented some strange antigravity technology that some say is, "out of this world".



The United States Air Force started secretly studying UFOs in 1940s under projects Sign (1947), Grudge (1949), and Blue Book (1952) to determine if they were a threat, and to scientifically analyze UFO-related data. Most UFO cases were explained, however, A small percentage of UFO reports were classified as unexplained, even after expert analysis. Historically, the US government denied the existence of advanced aircraft and frequently orchestrated cover-ups to mislead the public.




Things finally changed December 16th 2017 when the Advanced Aerospace Threat Identification Program (AATIP), a secret program to study Unexplained Aerial Phenomena (UAP), was made public. That same day, the 2004 Nimitz UFO video was leaked to the media, though the US Navy didn't confirm the videos authenticity until 2019.


According to Commander David Fravor, the 2004 Nimitz Encounter UFO traveled around 60 miles in a second without breaking the sound barrier, had no known propulsion or exhaust, and actively jammed radar.




In 2016 the US Navy quietly filed patent applications for a "mass reduction device", which was approved in 2017. The patent claims microwave emitters creating high frequency electromagnetic waves can "vibrate in an accelerated mode and create a local polarized vacuum outside the outer resonate cavity wall".



This non-gravitational vacuum would allow the craft to become a hybrid aerospace/undersea craft (HAUC) capable of extreme underwater speeds .

"Lack of water-skin friction and enhanced stealth capabilities (non-linear scattering of RF and sonar signals), this hybrid craft would move with great ease through the air/space/water mediums, by being enclosed in a vacuum plasma bubble/sheath, due to the coupled effect of EM field-induced air/water particles repulsion and vacuum energy polarization."





So, advanced aircraft are real, but who is flying these UFOs? Extraterrestrials, military, or maybe both? Edgar Mitchell, the sixth man to walk on the moon, claims discs crashed, alien bodies were recovered, and technology was reverse engineered. He goes on to say that most advanced aircraft witnessed today are from a secret branch of the military.




Each person should think for themselves, do their own research, and draw their own conclusions. Together, we can debate, share ideas, and search for the truth.
