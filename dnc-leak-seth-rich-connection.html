DNC Emails Were Leaked Not Hacked: The Seth Rich Connection

by Watchful Watcher - February 27 2020


Follow @watchfulwatcher


The first phase of Julian Assange's extradition hearing opens in a London court on February 24th, leaving many to speculate on what information Assange has involving the Democratic National Committee email leak and Seth Rich.


Assange, the founder of WikiLeaks, has previously mentioned Seth Rich by name, and has stated multiple times that Russia was not the source of the DNC leak. If he can prove that the DNC emails were leaked rather than hacked by Russia, it could change the outcome of his extradition battle.


"If extradited and convicted in the United States, Assange faces up to 175 years in prison: 10 years for each of the 17 charges filed under the Espionage Act, and five years for a Computer Fraud and Abuse Act violation" - Committee to Protect Journalists



Who is Seth Rich?

Seth Rich was a 27 year old data analyst working on the DNC's voter expansion in 2016. On July 10th at approximately 4 a.m. he was shot in the back twice after a struggle in front of his home. He was taken to the hospital and was pronounced dead shortly after. The D.C. police suspect it was a failed robbery due to the high number of related robberies in the area, however, none of his belongings were taken.

DNC emails

On July 22 2016 WikiLeaks released nearly 20,000 US Democratic National Committee emails, part one of their, "Hillary Leak" series. In the following months the DNC leak would ultimately total 2 publications, 44,053 emails, and 17,761 attachments.


The DNC emails revealed large scale DNC bias against Bernie Sanders, and exposed Hillary Clinton's corrupt pay-to-play nature.

Seth Rich connection

So, how does Seth Rich fit into all this? The FBI and the mainstream media push the narrative that in the summer of 2016, Russia secretly planted a fake report claiming DNC staffer Seth Rich was assassinated, leading to conspiracy theories later promoted from inside President Trump's White House.


The 2017-2019 Special Counsel Investigation concluded that Russia hacked the DNC and leaked their emails to influence the 2016 presidential election. Special prosecutor Robert Mueller claims that Julian Assange used the murder of Seth Rich in an effort to cover up the fact that Russian Intelligence had hacked the DNC emails and transferred them to WikiLeaks.


During an interview on August 9th, 2016, Julian Assange mentioned Seth Rich while explaining how high the stakes are for WikiLeaks sources in the US. That same day, WikiLeaks decided to issue a $20k reward for information leading to the conviction for the murder of DNC staffer Seth Rich.

Later that month former California Rep. Dana Rohrabacher met with Assange at the Ecuadorian Embassy in London to discuss the DNC leak. Rohrabacher states that during the meeting Assange claimed that the materials he obtained and made public did not come from the Russians, and that he had more information he was willing to share with President Trump.



Craig Murray claims

In December, 2016, Craig Murray, former British ambassador to Uzbekistan and associate of Julian Assange, told the Dailymail that he flew to Washington D.C. for the DNC emails. He claims he had a clandestine hand-off in a wooded park near American University with a leaker that was disgusted with the corruption of the Clinton Foundation.



Bill Binney analysis

CIA director Mike Pompeo met with NSA whistleblower William Binney on October 24th, 2017, after President Trump urged the CIA director to meet with Binney to discuss his assessment that the DNC data theft was an inside job.


Binney's expert analysis concluded that on July 5th, 2016, data was leaked, not hacked, by a person with physical access to a DNC computer. His findings suggest that the DNC data was copied onto a storage device at speeds that far exceed an internet capability for a remote hack.

Donna Brazile

In late 2017, former interim head of the Democratic National committee Donna Brazile dedicated her book to "patriot" Seth Rich, whose death she claims made her fear for her own life. In her book she claims she shut the blinds in her office window so snipers couldn't see her.


This could be an attempt to increase her book sales, but Brazile's paranoia may be warranted. A leaked email from Hillary Clinton's 2016 presidential campaign chairman John Podesta states, "I'm definitely for making an example of a suspected leaker whether or not we have any real basis for it."

FBI cover-up

In 2018 FBI director James Comey directly intervened to kill Julian Assange's immunity deal. Assange reached out to the Trump administration in 2017, offering to not release CIA Vault7, and was willing to discuss technical evidence involving the DNC leak. Before the deal was finalized Comey sent a stand-down message to Assange's legal team without discussing the matter with Trump. WikiLeaks would go on to leak Vault7, the largest CIA leak in history.
